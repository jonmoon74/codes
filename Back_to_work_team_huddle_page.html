Having Conversations

Your people are returning to work and it’s entirely natural that they may feel apprehensive about this. They may also be hungry for information about what the new normal looks like on your site and for them specifically.
One really effective way of allowing your people to share how they are feeling and to provide them with specific guidance and support to help them get back to work is by setting up some team huddles.
The steps below will help you lead your team through effective team huddles.

1. Looking after yourself

Before you can effectively support your team with their concerns, you need to take care of yourself and your own concerns.
Use the buttons to access some of our range of resources to help you with mental health and wellbeing, as well as the importance of positivity.
